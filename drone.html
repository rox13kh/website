MORE ON DRONES

Drones are becoming increasingly prominent not only in the construction industry, but also in the military and the technology world in general. Also known as unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV), drones are enhancing construction work like no other technology available. They can help detect and prevent potential building problems, avoiding any schedule hindrances. A typical drone today can hover in one position for a significant amount of time without losing much stability. Slick and light, drones (and minidrones) have the ability to do monitoring and inspection that was done before with large flying bodies such as helicopters and smaller engine planes. It's stability and access to incredible heights has opened people's minds to what is possible with aerial photography and videography.
