SOFTWARE TRAINING

BUILD BETTER TOGETHER, BY UNDERSTANDING THE BUILDING PROCESS

Using incredibly advanced yet interactive software, both owners and contractors can now enhance their communication using the cloud.





DESIGNED FOR EVERYONE

Our curriculum aims to serve the building curiosities of every building team member. We want not only for the informed to deliver better, but also the uncritical to better understand what it takes to build the best structures.

PARAMETRIC MODELING

Learn from creating simple yet dynamic building components in 3D, unleashing your untouched creativity. 2D sketches can be slowly transformed to precise renderings, and later to assembly and fittings.

FLEXIBILITY

Use the .csv or IFC files in a spectrum of commercial construction software.

EFFICIENT AND EFFECTIVE COLLABORATION

Explore different options for the fundamental design, prefabrication, schedule and also costs through BIM. Using software like Revit, you can create bidirectional associativity, where any changes are automatically updated in the cloud. This improves teamwork, communication and also schedules.

REALISTIC MODELS

Not only can one create realistic 3D models, but also design virtual neighborhoods. They provide immersive experience, where virtual navigation of structures is possible. Add 3D models of people, trees and streets by using software like Sketchup PRO.

REAL-TIME 3/4/5D MODELING

Effective communication is an integral part of the process, hence using the cloud in BIM becomes indispensible. Learn to alter specific parts of the project in real-time with your collaborators, so better design decisions can be made.

HOLISTIC APPROACH AND CONTROL

Simulate projects, optimize costs and improve schedules by using popular software such as Navisworks and Vico Office. You can understand the whole building process — from design, scheduling, pricing to crew sizes.

MORE ON SOFTWARE TRAINING

Learn to simulate realistic neighborhoods, alter specific parts of the building in real-time, and also to optimize buildings' finances and schedules. Companies today invest more on training for better construction work. Our curriculum provides the widest range of softwares. By understanding the construction process, you are able to create stronger relationships with people working on and off-site. This will help enhance the project's life cycle. Regardless of your position in the building team, everyone can work together to create even better, more innovative projects in the future. Training is essential, as it teaches the group to be more collaborative. The members of the building group can learn each others' languages to share ideas and provide effective feedback.
